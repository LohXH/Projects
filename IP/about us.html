About Us



StylishNotts--AN ONLINE CLOTHING SITE

StylishNotts is an online clothing site established in 2021, Malaysia. We create endless style possibilities through an ever-expanding range of products with affordable prices.



QUALITY PRODUCTS, AFFORDABLE PRICES

Want to look style and fashion without burning a hole in your wallet? Check out our homepage for our best selling products and a catalogue of products which is convenient for you to look for apparels that meet your demands.



WIDE RANGE OF CLOTHINGS SIZE

Worried of clothings size from online store? We have size chart provided with all the measurements as reference to help you find the perfect fit. Our clothings size are available from XS to 4XL.











OUR DESIGNER

The main designer of StylishNotts, Virgil from the United States of America is a well-known designer and artistic director. He has received the Urban Luxe award at the 2017 British Fashion Awards. He also won International designer of the Year at the GQ men of the year awards in the same year. In 2018, he was listed as one of Time magazine's 100 most influential people in the world in 2018, which is one of the two designers named that year.
